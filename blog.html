Pet Shop

Title description, April 7, 2020

Pet stores are extremely popular in modern society. A pet shop or pet store is a retail business which sells different kinds of animals to the public. A variety of animal supplies and pet accessories are also available in pet shops. The products sold include: food, treats, toys, cat litter. Some pet stores provide engraving services for pet tags, which have the owner's contact information in case the pet gets lost.
