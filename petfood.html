Cats Food

Salmon

A good source of protein and omega-3 fatty acids, salmon can be a delicious and tasty treat for cats


Eggs

In order to reduce the risk of food borne diseases, be sure to cook any eggs you feed your cat. Eggs also can be found in many cat foods, such as Tiki Cat Koolina Luau chicken with egg in chicken consomme grain-free canned cat food.


Chicken

A good source of lean protein, chicken can be a great choice for your cat, as long as it is cooked thoroughly and the skin, which can be fatty, is removed before feeding it to your cat.


Cheese

If your cat shows an interest in this feline-friendly food, Wismer recommends feeding her a hard cheese, like cheddar, Swiss or Gouda. These cheeses are high in calcium and protein and can be easily baked into treats or fed to your cat raw in small amounts.
