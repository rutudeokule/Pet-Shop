Cats name with descriptions

Persian Cat

Persian Cat Breed Description: Long haired breed of cat that has a short round face with a wide head. It has a thick coat , large eyes and a shortened muzzle.
Average Lifespan: 12-17 Years


Scottish Fold

Breed Description: It is a medium sized cat weighing 4-6 kg. The coat can be long-haired or short-haired and ears can be normal or folded.
Average Lifespan: 11- 15 years


American Bobtail

Breed Description: These have a short to medium built body with short tails. Hips are as wide as chest. Ears are medium sized and eyes are almond shaped.
Average Lifespan: 11- 15 Years


Maine Coon

Breed Description: These are the largest breeds of domestic cats with soft and silky coat. They are easy to train and are a loyal breed.
Average Lifespan: 12- 15 Years


Tabby Cat

Breed Description: It is a domestic cat that has a shape of M on its forehead formed by the combination of stripes and patterns present on its coat.
Average Lifespan: 15- 20 years

Dogs name with description

Akita

The Akita is distinctive large and powerful dog with an aloof attitude.
The average lifespan of an Akita is 11 to 15 years.


Basset Hound

The Basset Hound is a medium-sized domesticated dog with short, muscular legs and a pair of long drooping ears. Basset Hounds are very long in the body. In fact, they are longer than they are tall and their legs have the thickest bones of any dog of this size.
Lifespan: 11-12 years


Beagle

The Beagle is member of the hound group of domesticated dogs and its ancestry can be dated back 2,500 years. Similar in appearance to the Foxhound, the Beagle also has a very advanced sense of smell and were employed to track game like rabbits and deer. These days they are often employed by security and police forces to sniff out contraband and serve all over the world in this role.
Lifespan: 12-15 years


British Bulldog

The English Bulldog has fast become a popular family pet and their gentle but protective nature means that they are very personable and loyal family members. The breed requires a great deal of human contact and will show high levels of affection to their owners. If provoked or threatened by a stranger, the English Bulldog will bravely stand its ground and protect loved ones with determination.
Lifespan: 7-10 years


Havanese

The Havanese is a small toy breed of domesticated dog. It has a thick coat of either long and straight or curly fur, ears that droop to the side of its face, and a great deal of personality.
Lifespan: 12-15 years

Birds name with description

Cuckoos

Brood parasitism involves the cuckoo laying its egg in the nest of another bird. The owners of the nest bring the cuckoo chick up as one of their own, even after it has pushed the other chicks out of the nest.


Parrots

Parrots have strong, hooked beaks for breaking open seeds and nuts. The outer two toes of each foot face backwards, while the inner two toes face forwards. This is known as a ‘zygodactyl’ arrangement, and is found in other primarily tree-dwelling birds such as toucans and woodpeckers.
